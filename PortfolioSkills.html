Advanced Charts

Week 8

These are two more advanced charts using more indepth analysis and techniques.

This Graph looks to determine if there exists a relationship between the population density of people within a borough and a high level of knife crime in the area through regression analysis. And I found there is no determinable relationship hence the line of best fit being so small for the graph and having such a low R^2 value.

The next graph considers a different potential factor for London's Crime rates and that is the Index of Multiple Deprivations within boroughs, and is presented through the use of an interactive scatter plot.

The chart includes a tooltip which gives specfic information for every individual Borough to help the audience have their own better interpretation of the relationship and results of the graph.
